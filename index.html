About me

I am a London based freelance filmmaker, editor and animator. I began as a studio technician, in a sound studio where I was taught how to work with sound recording equipment, and managed to climb up the ladder to a point at which I found myself capable of handling a project from scratch to final production. Alongside, I came across different job opportunities such as corporate filmmaking, infographics and feature production which helped me expand my knowledge in a variety of areas from filming to editing and finally directing. In the past 15 years I have been working with a variety of companies and clients from TV channels to individuals as a freelancer. I have developed a solid understanding of what is required in order to stand out in a multimedia oriented market both from aesthetics and technical point of view.
